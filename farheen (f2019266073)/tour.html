Faisal Mosque
The Faisal Mosque is the national mosque of Pakistan, located in capital Islamabad. It is the sixth-largest mosque in the world and the largest within South Asia, located on the foothills of Margalla Hills in Pakistan's capital city of Islamabad. Shangrila lake
Lower Kachura Lake, also known as Shangrila Lake, is a lake located near the city of Skardu in Gilgit−Baltistan, Pakistan. Situated at a height of 2,500 metres, it has had an operational resort on its bank since 1983.
